## How It Works Now

### Backend Random Text Generation:
```
1. Takes word limit (25, 50, 100, etc.)
2. Selects unique words from 120+ word bank
3. No repetition - each word used only once
4. Works for any limit up to 500 words
5. Adds numbers/punctuation if requested
6. Applies case settings
```

### Word Bank Size:
- Previous: 5 sample texts (limited to ~50 words)
- Now: 120+ unique common words (supports 500+ words)

### Quality:
- ✅ No repeated words
- ✅ Natural vocabulary
- ✅ All options work (punctuation, numbers, case)
- ✅ Fast generation
